Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide high school students with an organized, interactive, and comprehensive learning platform. It allows students to explore subject-specific content in a chapter-by-chapter format, attend scheduled live sessions, and access recorded classes for flexible learning. Each subject features wiki-style educational material that deepens understanding and supports independent study. The platform also tracks student progress through a personalized dashboard that displays attended sessions, completed chapters, viewed materials, and overall learning progress. By combining structured curriculum content, interactive lessons, and progress tracking, this website aims to help students learn effectively, stay motivated, and achieve their academic goals.
